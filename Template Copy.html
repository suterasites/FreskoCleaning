What We Do

Our Flooring Services

From restoring tired timber to installing brand-new floors, we deliver results that last a lifetime.

Floor Sanding & Refinishing

Breathe new life into your existing hardwood floors. We sand back years of wear and apply premium finishes for a stunning, durable result.

  • Dustless sanding systems
  • Staining & colour matching
  • Oil & polyurethane finishes

Hardwood Floor Installation

New timber floor installation with precision and care. We work with a range of Australian hardwoods to suit your style and budget.

  • Solid & engineered hardwood
  • Subfloor preparation
  • Custom layouts & patterns

LVP & Laminate Installation

Affordable, durable, and beautiful. We install luxury vinyl plank and laminate flooring that looks and feels like the real thing.

  • Waterproof LVP options
  • Quick installation
  • Wide range of styles
